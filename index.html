Project Overview

Our machine learning model, developed with a pivot point model and 5 years of historical data, predicts stock price movements. It generates buy, sell, or hold signals based on live market data and provides detailed explanations for decision-making. The system also automates order placement and profit-booking according to user-defined profit targets and risk tolerance. Moreover, it aligns with UN Sustainable Development Goals 1, 4, 5, 8, 11, and 17 to promote economic empowerment and sustainable development.
